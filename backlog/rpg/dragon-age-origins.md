# Dragon Age: Origins

**Verdict: Strong fit.** Party of 4 with direct control, RTwP tactical combat, high-fantasy setting, toggleable isometric/chase camera. One of the most recommended games by Dungeon Siege fans for companion-driven party adventuring.

## At a Glance

| Criterion | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| View | Isometric (zoomed out) or 3D chase cam — toggleable on the fly |
| Party | Hero + 3 companions (large roster to recruit, swap at camp) |
| Combat | Real-time with pause, full tactical control of all party members |
| Setting | Dark high-fantasy (Thedas) — Blight, darkspawn, grey wardens |
| RPG depth | Origin stories, class/spec builds, companion approval, branching narrative |
| Platform | PC — Steam/GOG, runs great on modern systems |
| Year | 2009 |

## Strengths

- **Full party control** — position, issue commands, use abilities for all 4 members
- **Tactical camera** — zoom out to isometric for full battlefield overview, zoom in for action view
- **Companion depth** — each has unique backstory, approval system, romance options, personal quests
- **Build variety** — warrior/rogue/mage with specializations (Champion, Assassin, Blood Mage, etc.)
- **Meaningful choices** — origin story affects dialogue/reactions, major decisions shape the world
- **DLC campaigns** — Awakening expansion adds new companions, abilities, and a full storyline

## Weaknesses

- Party capped at 4 (smaller than IWD's 6 or DS's larger groups)
- No custom party — you pick from pre-written companions (unlike IWD)
- Some areas feel linear compared to open-world exploration of DS
- Aging graphics (2009) without mods

## DLC / Expansions Worth Getting

| DLC | Type | Worth it? |
|-----|------|-----------|
| **Awakening** | Full expansion | Yes — new story, companions, abilities |
| **The Stone Prisoner** (Shale) | Companion + quest | Yes — great companion, often free |
| **Return to Ostagar** | Quest | Optional — short, nostalgic |
| **Warden's Keep** | Quest + storage | Nice to have — party storage chest |
| Others | Minor items/quests | Skip unless completionist |
