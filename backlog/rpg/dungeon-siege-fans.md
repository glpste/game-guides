# Games for Dungeon Siege Fans

What DS fans look for: full party gearing, real-time group combat (no turns), fantasy adventuring, loot/exploration loop.

## Top Recommendations (from fan communities)

| Tier | Game | Why DS fans like it |
|------|------|---------------------|
| **Best fit** | Icewind Dale EE | Full 6-person custom party, dungeon crawl, loot, RTwP. Closest in spirit |
| **Best fit** | Pillars of Eternity 1/2 | Modern isometric, deep party management, tactical RTwP |
| **Best fit** | Drakensang (+River of Time) | Underrated, direct party control, strong loot/gear focus, RTwP |
| Strong | Dragon Age: Origins | Party of 4, RTwP, companion stories, high-fantasy tone |
| Strong | BG: Dark Alliance 1 & 2 | Isometric action, loot-heavy, hack-and-slash party feel |
| Good | Tyranny | Isometric RTwP, tight tactical combat, villain story |
| Good | Divinity: Original Sin 1 & 2 | Party freedom, creative combat, great exploration. **Turn-based** though |
| Good | NWN2: Enhanced Edition | Flexible camera, improved party control, huge mod support |
| Niche | Legend of Grimrock 1 & 2 | First-person real-time party dungeon crawler, traps/puzzles |
| Niche | Black Geyser | Indie isometric RTwP, old-school party control |

## Partial fits (lack true party control)

Solo-hero ARPGs with loot but no multi-character party management:
Titan Quest, Grim Dawn, Path of Exile, Sacred series
