## Key Insights
- **Sealed Strategy**: Force primaries around bombs/signposts; splash 3rd color with vivid/fixing (12+ sources). 23-24 lands, 15+ creatures, 6-8 removal. Aggro/tempo > midrange in mirrors.
- **Color Pairs**: Blue/Red > White/Blue/Green/Black strongest; avoid mono/3+ without fixing.
- **Data Caveat**: Early (low sample); track 17Lands/Untapped for updates. Primaries 55-60% winrate expected in tuned pools.
