# Lorwyn Eclipsed (ECL) Archetype Signposts and Bombs

## Archetype Signposts
Lorwyn Eclipsed features **five primary typal archetypes** (one per color pair from original Lorwyn block), each supported by gold signpost uncommons. These push tribal synergies with tribes like Merfolk, Goblins, Kithkin, Elementals, and Elves. Secondary archetypes exist around mechanics like Blight, Vivid, Flash/Faeries, but prioritize the primaries in Sealed.

| Archetype | Colors | Tribe/Theme | Signpost Uncommons | Playstyle Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------------|--------------------|-----------------|
| Merfolk (Tempo/Tap Value) | White-Blue (Azorius) | Merfolk + tapping triggers/convoke | Wanderwine Distracter, Silvergill Peddler, Deepchannel Duelist, Eclipsed Merrow | Disrupt opponents; trigger on tap (attack/convoke); value engines. |
| Goblins (Sac/Death) | Black-Red (Rakdos) | Goblins + Blight (-1/-1 counters) on self | Mudbutton Cursetosser, Boggart Cursecrafter, Eclipsed Boggart | Blight for triggers; sac/death payoffs; tokens for fodder. |
| Kithkin (Go-Wide Aggro) | Green-White (Selesnya) | Kithkin + ETB buffs/tokens | Clachan Festival, Mistmeadow Council, Timid Shieldbearer, Dundoolin Weaver, Thoughtweft Lieutenant, Eclipsed Kithkin | Wide boards; spread buffs; recover from wipes. |
| Elementals (Big Spells) | Blue-Red (Izzet) | Elementals + MV 4+ spells | Flamebraider, Twinflame Travelers, Eclipsed Flamekin | Ramp into bombs; double triggers; evoke value. |
| Elves (Graveyard Midrange) | Black-Green (Golgari) | Elves + self-mill/recursion | Scarblade Scout, Lys Alana Informant, Morcant's Loyalist, Eclipsed Elf | Fill yard; reanimate/proliferate; grindy value. |

**Secondary Signposts** (mechanic-based; splashable in primaries):
- UB Faeries/Flash: Voracious Tome-Skimmer
- RG Vivid/Aggro: Noggle Robber
- WB Blight/Value: Reaping Willow
- RW Blight/Aggro: Hovel Hurler
- GU Vivid/Ramp: Glister Bairn
