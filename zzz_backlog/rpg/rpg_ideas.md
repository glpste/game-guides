**Excluded:** Solo ARPGs (Diablo-likes), turn-based, locked first-person.

## Top Picks

1. **Icewind Dale EE** — closest to Dungeon Siege's seamless party adventuring + loot loop
2. **Pillars of Eternity** — modern take with more RPG depth

## Notes

- All entries allow camera control (zoom/rotate or true top-down)
- Most are under $10 on sale; look for Enhanced Editions for widescreen/UI/mod support
- Available on Steam and GOG; older titles run fine on modern PCs
