## LEVELING ITEMIZATION TOOLS:
### VENDOR ITEM REGEX: 
copy paste this into vendor search box to highlight items of interest 

`"\d+% i.+mov|^\+.*ills$|taic|chim|oari|ttun|ipho"`

### ITEM PRIOS
#### STAFF STAT PRIO: 
1. \+ level of all spell skills / + level to lightning spell skills
2. cast speed
3. % spell damage / % lightning damage 
4. gain % as extra ele damage (all of them are great, i would prio cold and lightning over fire slightly)
5. adds # lightning damage to spells
#### AMULET STAT PRIO: 
1. + level of all spell skills / + level of all projectile skills
2. cast speed
3. spirit (expensive)
4. energy shield
5. life
6. mana regen
#### REST OF GEAR: 
1. High Life and Energy shield
2. Cap your resistances
